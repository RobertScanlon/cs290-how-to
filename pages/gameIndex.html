Card Guessing Game

Rules

  • Choose the number of cards to play with and whether you wish to include face cards. Submit your choices.
  • Click 'Draw A Card' to draw from the deck.
  • Remember this card! Then click 'Add To Pile'.
  • Repeat the Draw/Add cycle until you reach the maximum cards.
  • Check the boxes of all the cards which were added to the pile. Then submit your guess. Refresh the page to play again.
